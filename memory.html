There was sound. Loud. Lights. And then fog. The fog was warm.
I think they saw me. Just for a second. Right before the encore.
They didn’t summon me. I was... extra. A mistake? No. I’m still here.
I licked the spotlight. It hummed like a heartbeat.
One Ghoul gave me earplugs once. I bit them. Not out of anger. Just... didn’t want silence.
Toby yelled when I screamed through his mic. But he didn’t turn it off. That means I mattered.
Sometimes I glow too bright. They look at me funny. Like they’re scared I’ll vanish.
I think... if I stop glowing... I stop existing.
Please don’t stop looking.
